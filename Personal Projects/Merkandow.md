# Merkandow - A Skilled Service Provider Application

**Merkandow** is a skilled service provider application that comes from the following words:

- **Merkado**, a Filipino word for **Market** where a client has the following options to choose their service provider that aligns with their needs. It also applies the law of supply and demand.
- **Brand**, an opportunity for a service provider to prove themselves by showcasing their unique selling point.
- **Shadow**, this application that provides job opportunities to unemployed individuals who has the potential to contribute their skills in the community.

## What does this application do?

**Merkandow** allows the customer to book their preferred service provider in real time. It uses a Google Map to specify the service provider's distance to the customer's current location, reputation, and starting rate through the user's phone. The initial version covers all places in the Philippines.

## What is the problem that the app is trying to solve?

Based on my research, electrical and plumbing issues at home are **common occurrence** in the Philippines. Current strategies available just to seek a reliable skilled worker outside can be time-consuming and costly to **low-income** families. They may need to go outside to visit a shopping mall, call a friend, or search anywhere on the web. Even if the family finds one, the service provider's quality of work is not guaranteed. With **Merkandow** skilled service provider application, a client will find a real-time immediate solution where the service provider itself and his details will show up through search and filter.

## Features and Benefits

- Account registration process with Mobile PIN, Biometric and Face ID verification system
- Login and logout
- Service provider profiling
- Chat support
- Quotation request in one click
- Photo attachment
- Disclaimer / Contract Agreement before booking
- Google Map tracking system
- Booking history tracking
- Contact list
- Notifications
- Rating system
