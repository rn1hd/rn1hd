# Mercandow - A Skilled Service Provider Application

**Mercandow** is a skilled service provider mobile application that allows the customer to book their preferred service provider in real time. It uses a Google Map to specify the service provider's distance to the customer's current location, reputation, and starting rate through the user's phone. The initial version covers all places in the Philippines.

## Why should the user care?

Based on my research, electrical and plumbing issues at home are **common occurrence** in the Philippines. Current strategies available just to seek a reliable skilled worker outside can be time-consuming and costly to **low-income** families. They may need to go outside to visit a shopping mall, call a friend, or search anywhere on the web. Even if the family finds one, the service provider's quality of work is not guaranteed. With **Mercandow** skilled service provider mobile application, a client will find a real-time immediate solution where the service provider itself and his details will show up through search and filter.

## Features and Benefits

- Account registration process with Fingerprint and Face ID verification system
- Login and logout system
- Service provider profiling
- Chat support
- Quotation request in one click
- Photo attachment
- Disclaimer / Contract Agreement before booking
- Google Map tracking system
- Booking history tracking
- Contact list
- Notifications
- Rating system
